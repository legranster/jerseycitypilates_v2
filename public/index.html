Why Pilates?

Pilates focuses on 9 Key Principles of the Pilates Method to help develop the mind, body, and spirit:

  • Breathing
  • Concentration
  • Control
  • Centering
  • Precision
  • Balanced Muscle Development
  • Rhythm/Flow
  • Whole Body Movement
  • Relaxation

Jersey City Pilates aims to use these principles to help benefit not only your Pilates workouts, but your lifestyle as well.
